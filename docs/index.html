About DJ Angerone

Mike Myers, professionally know as DJ Angerone, is internationally acclaimed Progressive House & Trance DJ & musician.

I try yo experiment with various music genres in every track that i make.

Since the beginning of his career in 2010, he has already released 5 EPs on a range of worldwide know lables (Booster Records, DSTRCT Recodings etc.) and received a number of music awards.
